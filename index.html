The NHS needs our help.

Many of us share a feeling that we'd pay a little more tax if we knew it was going to the NHS. In fact, this survey recently found that up to three-quarters of Brits would be happy to pay more to support the NHS. So let's do that!

Most NHS Trusts have a charity that you can easily set up monthly payments to, and the money will go straight into improving care in our NHS.

This page will help you figure out how much money an extra 1% of tax would be for you, and help you find the page for your local NHS trust so you can set up a monthly payment of that amount. If we all chipped in a percent or two, it wouldn't affect us too much, but it would mean the world for our NHS.

Please note that this page is in no way associated with the NHS. We've made every attempt to make sure that the information and links it provides are correct and safe, but we can't provide any guarantees of accuracy.
