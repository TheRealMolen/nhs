Let's re-fund our NHS!

Many of us share a feeling that we'd pay a little more tax if we knew it was going to the NHS. So what if we just made our own tax budget and made that happen?

This page will help you figure out how much money an extra 1% of tax would be for you, and help you find the page for your local NHS trust so you can set up a monthly payment of that amount. If we all chipped in a percent or two, it wouldn't affect us too much, but it would mean the world for our NHS.

This tool will help you calculate how much 1% extra tax would be for you, and will link you to NHS Trust charities that you can set up a monthly donation to.
